# NNNN. Short title of the decision

- **Status:** Proposed | Accepted | Superseded by NNNN
- **Date:** YYYY-MM-DD

## Context

What forces are at play? The problem, constraints, and requirements that make a
decision necessary. State facts, not the decision.

## Decision

The choice we made, stated plainly ("We will ...").

## Consequences

What becomes easier and what becomes harder as a result. Include the accepted
downsides — an honest ADR names its own costs.

## Alternatives considered

Each realistic option and why it lost (or when we'd revisit it).
